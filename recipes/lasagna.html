Lasagna

Description

This recipe is quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 30g pasta
  • 1 tin of tomatoes
  • 100g of butter
  • 1 cup of basil leaves, fresh
  • 2 cups of water
  • 100g of parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Bring 2 cups of water to the boil, adding 30g of pasta
  2. In a separate pan, melt 50g of butter, add 1 tin of tomatoes, and simmer for 5 minutes
  3. Once tomatoes have reduced, add 50 grams of parmesan
  4. Once pasta has cooked, drain and add remaining butter to pasta
  5. Add pasta to pan, combine all ingredients, including parmesan and basil
  6. Serve in a large bowl
