Scrambled Eggs

Description

Perfect scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 100g of butter
  • handful of parsley
  • 50ml full cream
  • 30g of parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. On a medium heat, melt 100g of butter
  2. In a bowl, whisk 4 large eggs with 50ml of cream
  3. Once butter has melted, add whisked eggs and cream
  4. Allow eggs to cook slightly, pushing the cooked eggs inwards to the center
  5. Continue pushing eggs towards the center until eggs are scrambled and cooked to your liking
  6. Add diced parsley and garnish with grated parmesan cheese
