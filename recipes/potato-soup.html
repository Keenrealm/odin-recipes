Potato Soup

Description

Warming winter potato soup

Ingredients

  • 3 large potatoes
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 100g of butter
  • handful of parsley
  • 500ml full cream
  • 30g of parmesan cheese
  • 300ml boiling water

Steps

  1. On a medium heat, melt 100g of butter in a large pot
  2. Peel and dice potatoes
  3. Add diced potatoes to pot, fry potatoes for 2 minutes, then add boiling water, simmer until soft
  4. Once potatoes are soft, add full cream and parmesan
  5. Once soup has returned to a simmer, blend all ingredients with stick blender until smooth
  6. Add diced parsley and garnish with grated parmesan cheese
